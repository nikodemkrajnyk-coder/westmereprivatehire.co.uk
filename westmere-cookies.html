Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This policy explains what cookies are, which ones we use on westmereprivatehire.co.uk, and how you can control them.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device to remember information about your visit — such as language preferences or whether you are signed in. Similar technologies include local storage and session storage.

2. Cookies We Use

NamePurposeDuration
Session storage (booking draft)Temporarily holds your pickup, destination, and time while you complete a booking so nothing is lost if you navigate away.Session only
Local storage (recent quotes)Remembers the last few fare quotes you viewed so the calculator loads faster next time.Up to 30 days
Google Fonts / MapsLoaded from Google servers so the site can render its typography and address autocomplete. Subject to Google's own privacy terms.Per Google policy

We do not currently use tracking or advertising cookies, and we do not share your data with third-party advertisers.

3. Managing Cookies

Most browsers let you block or delete cookies through their settings. Blocking essential cookies may prevent parts of the booking flow from working correctly. You can manage cookies in your browser settings — see aboutcookies.org for step-by-step guidance.

4. Changes to This Policy

If we change the cookies we use, we will update this page. Please check back periodically for the latest version.
