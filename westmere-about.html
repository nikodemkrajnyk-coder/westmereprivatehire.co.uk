Who We Are

Private hire, done properly.

Westmere began with a simple idea: private hire should feel personal. Based in Lewes, East Sussex (4 Fisher Street, BN7 2DG), we built this service around the standards we would want for ourselves.

That means a driver who knows your name, a car that is immaculate, and a confirmation sent the way you prefer — by phone, email or message. Every detail is handled before you step in.

We are not a fleet of anonymous drivers. You will know who is collecting you, and they will know where you are going. That continuity is what separates a genuine service from a transaction.

Our drivers are licensed, DBS-checked, and trained to deliver a calm, professional service every time. Discretion and punctuality are not optional — they’re the standard.

How We Work
