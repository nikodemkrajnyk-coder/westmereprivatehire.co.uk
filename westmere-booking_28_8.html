What We Offer

A Service
For Every Journey

Whether you need a pre-dawn airport run, a refined vehicle for a day of business, or a considered tour through the Sussex countryside — Westmere is at your service.

01
Airport Transfers
Stress-free journeys to all major UK airports. Flight tracking included — we monitor your flight and adjust pickup time if you're early or delayed.
02
Business Chauffeur
Professional, discreet transport for meetings, client travel and company events. A quiet, composed cabin — arrive focused and refreshed, every time.
03
General Private Hire
From hospital appointments to evenings out, school runs to days at the races — safe, comfortable and always on time.
04
Account Customers
Regular travellers and businesses can open a monthly account. Consolidated invoicing, priority booking and dedicated support.
05
Full-Day Chauffeur
Reserve the car and driver for the entire day — a single fixed daily rate, all-inclusive. Your driver waits, wherever you go. Parking charges settled separately and transparently at cost. Enquire to discuss your requirements.

Sussex Vineyard Tour
A private, chauffeured day through the rolling vineyards of Sussex — visiting two or three estates at a pace that suits you. Sit back, enjoy the landscape and the wine; we take care of the rest. A considered day out, quietly arranged.
