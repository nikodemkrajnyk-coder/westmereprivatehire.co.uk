Where we drive

All of Sussex and beyond

Westmere Private Hire is based in Lewes District, East Sussex, and covers the whole county — from the coastal towns of the south to the market towns of the north, and everywhere in between.

We specialise in fixed-fare airport transfers to all London airports including Gatwick, Heathrow, Stansted, Luton, Southampton and London City. No surge pricing, no meters — the fare quoted is the fare you pay.

Not sure if we cover your area? Call or message us directly — we are flexible and will always try to accommodate you.
